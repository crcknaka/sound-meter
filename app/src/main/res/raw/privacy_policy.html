Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sound Meter ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Sound Meter is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does NOT record or store any audio
  • Does NOT collect personal data
  • Does NOT require user registration
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT require internet connection

Microphone Permission

The app requires access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of measuring ambient sound levels in real-time. The audio data is:

  • Processed locally on your device only
  • Used exclusively to calculate decibel levels
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Immediately discarded after processing

Data Storage

Sound Meter does not store any measurement data permanently. All statistics (MIN, AVG, MAX values) are kept in temporary memory during your session and are cleared when you close the app or press the Reset button.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Sound Meter does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
